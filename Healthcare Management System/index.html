Our Specialities

Cardiology

Our cardiology department provides advanced heart care services including ECG, angiography, and heart surgery. We focus on prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of cardiovascular diseases using modern technology.

Dermatology

We offer expert skin care treatments for acne, allergies, infections, and cosmetic concerns. Our specialists use the latest techniques to ensure healthy and glowing skin.

Orthopedics

Our orthopedic team treats bone, joint, and muscle issues including fractures, arthritis, and sports injuries. We provide both surgical and non-surgical treatments for faster recovery.

Pediatrics

We provide complete healthcare services for infants, children, and adolescents including vaccinations, regular checkups, and treatment for common illnesses.
